Submit an Accessibility Feature

Let's start spreading positivity around accessibility! Look around you and share accessibility features that you like the most of any Product, Service, Digital - Web or Mobile, Built Environment, or Gaming.

Instructions

Though we have kept the form fairly simple, just some housekeeping.

  1. Category – Select the category of the Accessibility Feature.
  2. Spotlight Name – Give a short title of the accessibility feature that you want to share eg: Braille Menu Card.
  3. Description – Give a description of the accessibility feature. This is optional.
  4. Organization – Name of the organization that has implemented the Accessibility Feature.
  5. Country of origin – The country of origin /headquarters of the company whose accessibility feature you are mentioning.
  6. Reference Image – If you would like to share a screenshot or photograph it can be .jpg, jpeg, or png. Also, remember to provide the image description.
  7. Reference link – Provide a link to know more about the feature - remember to provide the link with http:// or https://
  8. Contributor Name & Email id – Do share your information, so we can recognize your contribution. We will also be recognizing Top Contributors! Don’t worry, we won’t share your email information on the website.

Happy GAAD & the BarrierBreak team would like to thank you for sharing accessibility features around you and help people realize the importance of accessibility & inclusion.
